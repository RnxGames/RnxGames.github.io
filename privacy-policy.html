R-Zen

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: July 2, 2026

Short version: R-Zen collects no personal data. Everything you do stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads, no analytics — ever. The app is free; an optional one-time purchase unlocks all content and is handled entirely by Apple / Google — we never see or store your payment details.

Who we are

R-Zen is developed and published by RnxGames. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at xnerzogames@gmail.com.

What we do NOT collect

  • No personal information (name, email, age, location)
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to any server
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No payment, card, or billing information (in-app purchases are processed entirely by the App Store / Google Play — see “In-app purchases” below)
  • No account registration of any kind

Data stored on your device only

R-Zen saves the following data locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere:

  • Your progress, completed puzzles, and mandala saves
  • XP, level, achievements, and streaks
  • Your preferences (language, theme, sound settings, orientation)
  • Photos you choose to use as puzzles (stored in your device's own file system)

Deleting the app removes all of this data permanently.

Camera and Photo Library

R-Zen includes an optional feature that lets you turn one of your own photos into a puzzle. To use it, the app will request access to your device camera or photo library.

  • Access is only requested when you choose to use this feature.
  • Photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted.
  • You may deny this permission — all other app features work without it.

In-app purchases

R-Zen is free to download and play. It offers a single, optional one-time purchase ($1.99) that unlocks all premium content (the full puzzle and mandala galleries and free-draw mode). There are no subscriptions and no other purchases; future updates are free.

  • The purchase is processed entirely by Apple’s App Store or Google Play. R-Zen never receives, sees, or stores your name, card number, or any billing information.
  • Whether you own the unlock is remembered only on your device; it is also tied to your App Store / Google account by the platform so you can restore it on your other devices via “Restore purchases”.
  • No purchase is required to use the app, and buying the unlock sends no personal data to us.

Fonts

R-Zen uses the Quicksand and Dancing Script typefaces. These fonts are bundled inside the app and loaded from your device — no requests are made to Google Fonts or any external font service.

Music and sounds

All music and ambient sound files are bundled inside the app. R-Zen does not connect to Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, or any other external music service. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

Third-party services

R-Zen does not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or social features. The app makes no network requests during normal use.

Children

R-Zen does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, the new version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will change. Since we collect no data, any changes would only reflect new features or clarifications, never new data practices.

Contact

Questions? Write to us at xnerzogames@gmail.com.
